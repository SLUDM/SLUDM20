Events

Main Event, FTKolor Run, Miracle Family Events

Events Summary

Saint Louis University Miracle Network Dance Marathon hosts numerous events throughout the year to support local Children’s Miracle Network Hospitals. SLUDM has two primary events. Our FTKolor Run which is a 5k held in the spring used to kickstart our fundraising for the year and our Main Event which is a 12 hour long Dance Marathon. We also host numerous small events such as a car wash, game and craft nights at the hospital and more!

Main Event

Our Main Event is a twelve hour long Celebration! During the event we celebrate a year’s worth of hard work, time, commitment, passion, dancing and fundraising done by those involved in our organization. Our event consists of learning a Morale Dance, playing Miracle Cup Games, listening to Miracle Family stories, and watching performances from our SLU campus dance and acapella groups. Finally, we conclude our event with a Circle of Hope and a final fundraising total reveal! Join us in being One Generation Fighting for the next, sign up today!

Miracle Family Events

Miracle Family Events are when we get to interact with our miracle families and kiddos, both the ones who are ambassadors for the hospital and ones who are being treated. These are events like hospital tours and craft nights with the kiddos!

Fundraising Events: Day of Miracles and May is for Miracles

SLUDM hosts two major fundraising events throughout the year, one in the spring and one in the fall though these events have similar layoyts their main purpose is fundraising and raising awareness. In the spring of 2019 we hosted our first ever May is for Miracles and raised just over $6,000 this was a great way to kick off our fundraising for the year! Day of Miracles occurs in November before our Main Event and last year we were able to raise over $49,000. Stay tuned for upcoming dates.
