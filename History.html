About Us

Saint Louis University Miracle Network Dance Marathon is one of over 300 Miracle Network Dance Marathon Programs across the country! We are a student organization located at Saint Louis University, consisting of 17 executive board members, over 70 CORE team members, and over 600 Miracle Makers, general paraticipants. As we enter into our 11th year, we continue to celebrate all the miracles and memories we have made, and we are thrilled to continue creating these! All of our paricipants have one main thing in common, our mission. Our mission is to raise funds and awareness for child healthcare!
