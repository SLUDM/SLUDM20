What is a Mini Marathon Program?

Overview

Our Mini-Marathon Programs are an extension of Saint Louis University Miracle Network Dance Marathon. These programs work closely with our High School Programming Chair to create programs at their schools. All of the funds they raise benefit SSM Health Cardinal Glennon and Saint Louis Childrenâ€™s Miracle Network Hospital. SLUDM works hard to provide each of our Mini-Marathons with the resources, support and guidance they need to create amazing events. These programs help to spread the mission of SLUDM as well as help to make more Miracles Happen For The Kids.

2019 Total Reveals!

Collegiate School of Medicine and Bioscience

The committed and passionate group of CSMB students was able to raise $7,468.90 in 2018 and 2019 For The Kids! We are so proud and we can't wait to see what more they can accomplish they do in the future!

Clayton High School

We loved partnering with Clayton High School in their first ever Dance Marathon and are so happy to still be able to support them now! With their dedication, excitement, and hardwork in 2019 and 2020, they raised $47,838.18 For the Kids!

Francis Howell North

The dedicated group of students at Francis Howell North High School did an amazing job putting on their second Dance Marathon in 2019 and were able to raise over $1,300 For The Kids. We are so proud of their hard work and evrythign they accomplished, and look forward to the miralces they will make in the future!

Mary Institute and Saint Louis Country Day School (MICDS)

We enjoyed helping MICDS host their first ever Dance Marathon with three separate ones as their school is K-12, and look forard to continuing to help them succeed! Seeing all of the passionate elementary, middle, and high students be FTK was truly inspiring as they raised $1,512.09 in 2019 and 2020!

Parkway Central High School

These awesome students had their first Dance Marathon in 2020 and were able to raise $25,085.16 For the Kids! They are going into their second Dance Marathon in 2021 and we are excited to support them in making miracles happen!
