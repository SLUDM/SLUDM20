Children's Miracle Network Hospitals

Our Mission and Miracle Families

Saint Louis University Miracle Network Dance Marathon strives to celebrate childhood and support the families of SSM Health Cardinal Glennon Children’s Hospital and St. Louis Children’s Hospital. Through raising funds and awareness for Children’s Miracle Network Hospitals of Greater St. Louis. By empowering students and the surrounding community to push boundaries and love intentionally, we will be one generation fighting for the next. As one movement, we share stories, inspire hope, and dance for more memories and miracles. We continue to support these hospitals’ efforts to fund resources that enhance childhood and make the hospital environment conducive for learning, growing and healing.

Miracle Families and Kiddos represent the true heart of our mission! We are fortunate to be able to spend time with our Miracle Families during our events, hearing their stories, doing crafts, and playing games! Through the years we have been able to watch our Miracle Kiddos grow and experience new adventures! To learn more about our Miracle Kiddos, click below!

Our Two Local Children's Miracle Network Hospitals

SSM Health Cardinal Glennon Children's Hospital

  • This incredible Children’s Miracle Network Hospital started with humble beginnings and has now grown into a nationally ranked CMN Hospital. It has over 190 inpatient and outpatient beds, and cares from children of all ages from the surrounding St. Louis area, Illinois and across the United States. They have amazing pediatric heart center, NICU, Fetal care, Gastroenterology center, Ronald McDonald House and are recognized as one of the best Children’s hospitals for Cardiology, Gastroenterology, and Neonatology in the Midwest.
Virtual Tour

Saint Louis Children's Hospital

  • This Children’s Miracle Network Hospital has a mission of doing what’s right for the kids, they live out this mission by providing safe and effective care to all their patients and families everyday. This nationally accredited Children’s Hospital has treated children of all ages from all 50 states and approximately 80 other countries. They house incredible NICU facilities and a cerebral palsy center. They are nationally ranked for their transplant program, epilepsy care, cerebral palsy therapies and asthma and burn care.
